Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

Ingredients:

    1. Mix dry Ingredients.
    2. Add wet Ingredients.
    3. Bake at 180 Celsius for 35 minutes.
